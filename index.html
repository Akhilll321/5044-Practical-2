How to interact

  • Select countries. Click a country on the map to pin it; click again to un-pin. Up to three countries can be compared at once — the first appears in orange, the second in indigo, and the third in purple. This colour convention is shared across the map, radar and bar chart.
  • Countries without breastfeeding data are shaded grey and cannot be selected — hovering still shows their tooltip (marked "No breastfeeding data").
  • Population overlay. Use Display style to render under-5 population as a pictogram, bubble, or hide it entirely. For pictograms, Icon unit controls how many people each figure represents.
