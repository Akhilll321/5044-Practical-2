How to interact

  • Select countries By clicking a country on the map to pin it and clicking again to remove the pin. Up to three countries can be compared at once, with the first appears in orange, the second in indigo, and the third in violet. These colours are consistent across the map, radar and bar chart.
  • Hover over dashboard elements to display additional information about specific countries.
  • Countries without breastfeeding data are shaded grey and cannot be selected but hovering will still show their tooltip (marked "No breastfeeding data").
  • Use Display style to render the map with a pictogram or bubble overlay, or remove a current overlay. For pictograms, Icon unit controls how many people each figure represents. Icon unit controls how many people each figure represents.
  • Views are linked. so selecting a country on the map will update the radar and bar chart to show that country's data.
